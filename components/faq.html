What services do you offer?

I specialize in penetration testing, vulnerability assessments, bug bounty hunting, and security audits. I work with web applications, APIs, cloud infrastructure, and mobile applications.

How do I engage your services?

Contact me via the form below or email directly. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your security needs, scope, timeline, and deliverables.

What is your turnaround time?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A typical web application assessment takes 1-2 weeks. Rush projects can be accommodated with advance notice.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, absolutely. All client information and findings are kept strictly confidential. I'm happy to sign NDAs and work agreements before any engagement begins.

What's in the final report?

You'll receive a comprehensive report including an executive summary, detailed findings with CVSS scores, proof-of-concept exploits, and actionable remediation recommendations.

Do you provide remediation support?

Yes! I provide technical guidance, code reviews for fixes, and retesting services to verify vulnerabilities have been properly addressed.
