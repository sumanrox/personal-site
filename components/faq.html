Everything you need to know about my security services

What services do you offer?

I specialize in penetration testing, vulnerability assessments, bug bounty hunting, and security audits. I work with web applications, APIs, cloud infrastructure, and mobile applications.

How do I engage your services?

Contact me via the form below or email directly. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your security needs, scope, timeline, and deliverables. All engagements include a detailed report with findings and remediation steps.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A typical web application assessment takes 1-2 weeks. Smaller bug bounty-style tests can be completed within days. Rush projects can be accommodated with advance notice.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, absolutely. All client information and findings are kept strictly confidential. I'm happy to sign NDAs and work agreements before any engagement begins.

What information will I receive in the final report?

You'll receive a comprehensive report including an executive summary, detailed findings with CVSS scores, proof-of-concept exploits, risk assessments, and actionable remediation recommendations. All findings are prioritized by severity and business impact.

Do you provide remediation support?

Yes! I provide technical guidance, code reviews for fixes, and retesting services to verify vulnerabilities have been properly addressed. Extended support packages are also available for ongoing security consultation.
